Hippocampus 

Companion object Hippocampus

trait Hippocampus extends AnyRef

The headquarters of the short term memories and of the management of the memory in general.

It takes care of the creation of new short term memories, the updating of their status and it moves the most frequent memories to the neocortex, creating long term memories.

It is also the responsible of the management of memories during a search, choosing the best memories.
